About The Scholar Companion Website

The **Scholar Companion** is a specialized, mobile-first application designed to be your essential academic co-pilot. Built to meet the high standards of modern engineering and technology curricula, this toolkit offers fast, accurate, and professional tracking of your most critical academic metrics.

**This streamlined application transforms complex institutional calculation rules (like attendance requirements and internal marks distribution) into simple, instant, actionable insights.** It is designed to help you stay ahead of your requirements and focus on your studies.

We are actively developing new features to integrate syllabus tracking and personalized timetable management, ensuring the tool continues to evolve with your academic needs.
